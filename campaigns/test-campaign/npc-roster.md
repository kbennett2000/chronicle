## Garrick
- **Description:** Stout gate guard in a mismatched breastplate, carrying
  a spear; tired but watchful.
- **Disposition:** Grateful and respectful — Kira nearly died investigating
  the tower when the town watch would not. Now an ally.
- **Knows:** The watchtower lights (blue-green, after dark, ~2 weeks). The
  tower's been empty for generations. Tobin the miller's boy vanished
  there two nights ago. Road north is safe by day. Points to Barrow at the
  Gilded Antler for the tower's history.

## Barrow
- **Description:** Broad, balding innkeeper of the Gilded Antler; wipes
  mugs with a rag; slow, deliberate speech. Older, a lifelong Millhaven man.
- **Disposition:** Friendly and talkative once a room is paid for. Wary of
  the tower but willing to share what he knows.
- **Knows:** The watchtower was a border beacon abandoned generations ago
  after a "bad winter." Local lore says it was sealed for a reason. The
  blue-green lights are new and unnatural. Worried for Tobin, the miller's
  boy who vanished there.
