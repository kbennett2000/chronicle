# Quest Log

## Active
- **Strange lights at the watchtower** — rumor brought Kira to Millhaven.
  Per Garrick: blue-green "witchfire" lights began ~a fortnight ago, only
  after dark. The tower has stood empty for generations. Two nights ago
  Tobin, the miller's boy, went up on a dare and never returned. The road
  north through the Greywood is safe by day, dangerous after dark. Barrow,
  keeper of the Gilded Antler, knows the tower's history.
  - First tower foray (day 2): reached the first landing, dodged a 7th-step
    crossbow tripwire, but was driven off at 1/16 HP by a gloom-touched
    giant spider lairing behind a warped door above the landing. Fled and
    recovered in Millhaven.
  - Day 3: returned north carefully, climbed past the sprung tripwire, and
    opened the second-floor guardroom. DISCOVERY: the "witchfire" is a
    gloom-corruption bleeding from the spider's tainted egg sacs and silk,
    not cast magic. TOBIN FOUND ALIVE — cocooned and being drained to feed
    the eggs.
  - **Tobin RESCUED (day 3):** Kira cut him free mid-fight and carried him
    out to Millhaven; the spider missed its parting strike and won't leave
    the dark. Tobin is alive but feverish and corruption-touched, tended by
    his family. The immediate missing-boy crisis is resolved.
  - STILL OPEN: the tower witchfire persists and the corrupted giant spider
    and its tainted egg-brood remain in the guardroom. To fully end the
    threat: destroy/cleanse the egg sacs and deal with the spider. Whatever
    corruption caused this (and what it did to Tobin) is unexplained.
