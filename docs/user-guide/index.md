# Welcome to Chronicle — Your Personal Dungeon Master

Imagine having a creative, patient Dungeon Master who is always ready to play, remembers every detail of your world, and lives quietly on your own computer.

That’s Chronicle.

No subscriptions to manage every month. No strangers on the internet. Just you, your stories, and a storyteller who knows your campaign inside and out.

## How It Works (the simple version)

1. You run Chronicle once on your **computer** (Windows, Mac, or Linux).
2. You open a browser on your **phone or tablet** on the same Wi-Fi.
3. The beautiful leather-and-parchment interface appears.
4. You talk to your Dungeon Master by typing what your character does — or tap suggested actions.
5. The storyteller weaves the tale, tracks your character sheet, updates the world, and remembers everything.

That’s it. Your computer does the heavy thinking. Your phone is just the window into the story.

## What You Need

- A Windows, Mac, or Linux computer
- A phone or tablet on the **same home Wi-Fi network**
- 20–30 minutes the first time you set it up
- A **Claude account** — a Claude Pro or Max subscription powers the storyteller (we’ll help you sign in; it only takes a moment)
