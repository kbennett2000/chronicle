# Chronicle

**Your personal, private Dungeon Master — right on your own computer.**

Chronicle is a beautiful, mobile-first solo D&D 5e app powered by a wise AI storyteller who remembers everything about your campaign. No internet required after setup. Your adventures stay completely private on your home network.

Think of it as having a dedicated Dungeon Master who is always available, never gets tired, and keeps perfect continuity across every session.

## Get Started (takes about 20–30 minutes the first time)

Chronicle works on **Windows, Mac, and Linux**.

Choose your computer below and follow the simple, picture-guided steps:

- 🪟 **[I use Windows](docs/user-guide/install/windows.md)**
- 🍎 **[I use a Mac](docs/user-guide/install/mac.md)**
- 🐧 **[I use Linux](docs/user-guide/install/linux.md)**

Once the storyteller is running on your computer, you’ll open Chronicle on your phone or tablet like opening a favorite book. The interface feels like a leather-bound journal with warm parchment pages and candlelight.

## What You’ll Need

- A Windows, Mac, or Linux computer (laptop or desktop)
- A phone or tablet on the **same home Wi-Fi**
- About 20–30 minutes the first time
- A free “magic key” from Anthropic (we walk you through getting one — it takes 2 minutes and you only pay for what you actually use)

## After Setup

- Play entirely from your phone or tablet
- Create as many campaigns as you like
- The storyteller remembers every detail, NPC, location, and quest
- Optional: Turn on beautiful illustrations that appear as new characters and places are discovered

Your stories stay safe and private — nothing ever leaves your home network.
